Pay to Win?

This data set is comprised of exactly 341 individual player records for the 2016-2017 NBA Season. The visual provided compares the average points per game each player recorded that season versus the player salaries (in millions). As you can see there is a clear and positive correlation between player performance and their annual salaries. Players like LeBron James, James Harden and Russel Westbrook were statistical fits. While players like Isaiah Thomas and Tristian Thompson were statistical outliers.
